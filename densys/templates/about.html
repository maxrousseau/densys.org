Purpose

The digital age brings with it new opportunities for the dental and medical fields. Complex data types often requires software to allow researchers to better understand underlying phenomena. Scientific computing has become ubiquitous in most fields studying nature. Hence, the importance of open source software in reasearch has grown considerably.

Just like the large archives of scientific papers we wish to create a platform to highlight and promote useful open source projects aimed at scientific discovery.

Densys presents projects submitted by researchers with the objective of increasing accessibility to and visibility for scientists.

We accept a wide variety of projects, from machine learning models, databases to scripts or fully featured software projects. For more information, on how to contribute your work to Densys see the contributing section.

Contributing

Submiting your software is quite simple. We ask that a short article be written to describe what the software does, the publications or posters that are associted with it and information on how to get the software. Please send this information via email to the this address.
